About Me

Hello there! I’m Hartley Scales, the creative mind behind HMS Web Developement, based right here in Chicago. Being 16 might seem young, but in the web development world, it’s my superpower. Why? Because I grew up with the internet as my playground, which means I naturally understand what makes a website truly click with people today.

At HMS Web Developement, I offer something special: websites that are not just beautiful to look at but are also a breeze to use. I believe in making sites that everyone loves to visit, using my up-to-the-minute knowledge of the latest digital trends. This isn’t just my job; it’s what I love to do, and I bring that enthusiasm to every project I take on.

Running this agency by myself lets me give every client my full attention. I dive deep into understanding what you need and work with you to make that happen. From the first sketch to the final click, I’m there, making sure your site stands out in the crowded online world.

Looking for a web developer who gets the digital age like only a digital native can? You’ve found him. At HMS Web Developement, let’s turn your digital dreams into reality. Ready to start? I can’t wait to hear from you.
